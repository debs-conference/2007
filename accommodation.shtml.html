Conference Venue

Please note that DEBS'07 does not have a block of rooms set aside with any hotel. Please make your own hotel arrangements. Below, we list hotel categories and suggested accomodations in each category. Our suggestions draw from hotels that are in close vicinity of the conference venue:

Bahen Center for Information Technology
University of Toronto
40 St. George Street (map)
Toronto, Ontario M5S 3G4

Please also note that June is a busy month for many hotels in Toronto, so plan ahead and book early. DEBS'07 cannot help you with room reservations.

Accommodations

We will provide information for three different hotel categories:

  • High-end (between $CAD 120 and 350)
    • Quality Hotel Midtown Toronto
    • Delta Chelsea
    • InterContinental Toronto Yorkville
  • Medium-end (around $CAD 100)
    • 89 Chestnut
  • Low-end (around $CAD 30, ideal for students)
    • New College Residence

The listed price ranges are approximate and are subject to change. The may also vary with availability. You may also want to use hotel reservation services like Octopus Travel or hotels.com to find a hotel in Toronto (which could be even cheaper in some cases).
