Registration

Registration to the DEBS conference includes participation in all conference sessions (5 invited speakers and keynotes, paper presentations, and panel), coffee and lunch breaks, a banquet, and a conference reception.

To register, please download and complete the registration form , and fax the signed form to +1-416-978-6876.
